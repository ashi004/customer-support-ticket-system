# Day 1 Notes – March 7, 2026 (Udemy Sections 1–3)

- Cloud Computing: On-demand delivery of IT resources over the internet (no upfront hardware).
- 6 Advantages: Agility, Scalability, Elasticity, Reliability, Cost savings (pay-as-you-go), Global reach.
- AWS Global Infrastructure: Regions (isolated geographic areas), Availability Zones (AZs – multiple data centers per Region), Edge Locations (for low-latency CDN).
- Why Cloud Practitioner: Entry-level cert to understand AWS basics, no deep coding required.
- Exam: CLF-C02 – 65 questions, 90 mins, multiple choice/response.
